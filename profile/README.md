Genomic research has shown us that sexual populations carry vast, functional, genome-wide variation that defies oversimplification. To address this complexity, biology must evolve.

In our labs, we are working to create this **new biology**. Our approach integrates:

* **Experimental Evolution**
* **Genome-Wide Sequencing and Analysis**
* **Large-Scale Bioinformatics**
* **New Theoretical Frameworks in Evolutionary Genetics**
